About Us

Meet the Owner

Bilal Mohamed

ElegantManke was born from a passion for quality menswear and a belief that every man deserves to look his best. Founded by Bilal Mohamed, the shop has grown into one of Eastleigh's most trusted destinations for elegant, well-fitted men's clothing.

We stock a carefully selected range of suits, shirts, trousers, and accessories — all chosen with the modern Nairobi gentleman in mind. Whether you need an outfit for the office, a special occasion, or everyday style, we have something for you.
