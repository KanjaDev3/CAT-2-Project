The client is an Eastleigh, Nairobi-based businessman who owns a men’s wear shop specializing in elegant and fashionable male clothing, including both casual and official outfits. The main purpose of the webpage is to create an online presence for the business, allowing customers to view products, learn more about the shop, and easily make inquiries or orders.

The website is intended to reflect a premium and stylish brand image. The client prefers a clean and modern design with a color scheme of white, black, maroon, and brown to match the elegance and masculinity of the brand.

The project focuses on improving visibility, attracting more customers, and making the business more accessible through a professional online platform.
